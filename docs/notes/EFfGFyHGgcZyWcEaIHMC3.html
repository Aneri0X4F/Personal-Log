Quantum Mechanics:

Set of principles and laws that govern systems of atomic and subatomic entities.

  • Bohr model represents an early attempt to describe electrons in atoms, in terms of both position (electron orbitals) and energy (quantized energy levels).
  • Wave-mechanical model: in which the electron is considered to exhibit both wave-like and particle-like characteristics.
  • Principle quantum numbers: K, L, M, N, O
  • Shape of electron subshell: s, p, d, f

Pauli exclusion principle

  • Each electron state can hold no more than two electrons, which must have opposite spins.
  • Thus, s, p, d, and f subshells may each accommodate, respectively, a total of 2, 6, 10, and 14 electrons.
