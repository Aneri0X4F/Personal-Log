**Motif** = atoms or group of atoms attached to form crystal structure
## Coordination Number** = number of nearest neighbours
* SC - 6
* BCC - 8
* FCC - 12
## Atomic packing factor
APF = Vol of atoms in 1 unit cell/ Total vol of unit cell
SC = 52% = (PI/6)
BCC = 68% = root 3* pi/8
FCC = 0.74%
